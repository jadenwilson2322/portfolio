FAQ

A few things borrowers ask us.

Are you the Department of Education?

No — RepayWisely is an independent service that helps you use federal programs correctly. You keep control of your accounts.

Can you guarantee forgiveness?

No one can guarantee forgiveness. We help you stay eligible, documented, and on the right plan.

What do I need for the call?

Have your studentaid.gov login and your loan servicer login handy so we can review your real numbers.
